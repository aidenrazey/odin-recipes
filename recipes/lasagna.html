Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is bloody incredible, to be honest.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and peppers to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Method

  1. Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Mix cheeses and meat with the noodles in alternate layers
